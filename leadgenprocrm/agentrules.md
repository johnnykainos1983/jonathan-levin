Section 1: Storage & Hygiene 🧹

500MB Threshold: Before any operation estimated to exceed 500 MB (builds, package installs), stop and ask for a "Go Ahead." ⚖️

Auto-Cleanup: Automatically delete dist, build, and temporary log files once a task is successfully completed. 🗑️

Size Reporting: Report the total workspace size (in MB/GB) before and after creating new files or folders. 📉
